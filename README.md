# Product-Demand-Insight-Dashboard
Product Demand Insight Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze and visualize product demand patterns, sales performance, and fulfillment efficiency.
## Features
## KPI Dashboard
Total Demand
Total Units Sold
Average Price
Total Inventory Level
##  Demand Trend Analysis
Time-based analysis of product demand
Identification of seasonal patterns and fluctuations
#Product Performance Analysis
Comparison of product demand and sales
Identification of top and low-performing products
## Regional Analysis
Demand distribution across regions
Identification of high-demand locations
## Top Products Table
Ranked list of products by demand
Includes units sold, price, and inventory levels
## Interactive Filters (Slicers)
Region 
Category
Seasonality
## Tools & Technologies
Power BI
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Power Query
CSV Dataset
## Dataset
The dataset used in this project (demand_forecasting.csv) contains:
Product demand records
Units sold data
Pricing information
Inventory levels
Regional and categorical attributes
Time-based sales/demand data
## Key Insights
Certain products consistently generate higher demand than others
Demand varies significantly across regions
Inventory levels do not always align with demand trends
Seasonal patterns influence product demand
Pricing differences may impact demand and sales performance
## Skills Demonstrated
Data cleaning and transformation using Power Query
Creating DAX measures for business KPIs
Building interactive Power BI dashboards
Data visualization and storytelling
